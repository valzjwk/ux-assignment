Events

Experience the Magic of Music

Music Odyssey

Music Odyssey is an annual concert held in January every year organised by NYP Symphony Orchestra. A group of Year 1s students would be chosen to plan for each year’s Music Odyssey. This will not only help the year 1 students grow as an individual but also bond as an orchestra.

Rhythm Resilience

Rhythm Resilience is a bi-annual performance organised by NYP Arts CCAs. CCAs like Chinese Cultural Group, Chinese Orchestra, Danz Inc, Dance Company and Foreign Bodies come together to host this performance for members of the public.

NYP Open House

NYP Open House is an annual event organised by NYP to give secondary school students a glimpse into Polytechnic’s life. With CCA performances, students can get an understanding of each of the CCAs offered in NYP. Not forgetting each of the school’s booths. Secondary school students can ask lecturers and current polytechnic students to better understand their desired courses.

Singapore International Band Festival (SIBF)

Singapore International Band Festival (SIBF) is an annual competition held in Singapore with participants from Taiwan, China, Vietnam, Thailand and Singapore! SIBF goes by a grading system where bands are graded upon a 100. In order to get a bronze award, the band needs to score 60 - 69. In order to get a Silver Award, the band needs to score 70 - 79. In order to get a Gold Award, the band needs to score at least 80 and above.

Grand Opus International

Grand Opus International is an International competition held in Ho Chin Min City every year in August. NYP Symphony Orchestra has been striving to get Gold Award through endless practices. As this is an International Competition, NYP Symphony Orchestra will go through auditions with our Conductor to determine who performs at Grand Opus International. As this is a grand opportunity, we wish everyone participating in this will give their serious efforts in rehearsals and sectionals.
