Overview

Nanyang Polytechnic Symphony Orchestra (NYPSO) established in 2002 with the purpose of bringing together people through music and self expression. Here, we encourage everyone, with or without musical background, to engage in the melodious dancing of harmonies to the euphonious sounds of music.

Our motto, ‚ÄúBe Bold, Unveil the Musical You‚Äù, inspires students to strive to express themselves through the language of music. Our CCA helps our members to find themselves through an orchestral experience, providing a supportive close knitted community of both alumni and instructors.



Our Story

2001

The idea of NYP Symphony Orchestra was conceived to connect students through western instruments.

2002

Official establishment of NYP Symphony Orchestra with four instrument sections.

Present

A 100-member strong orchestra with numerous achievements and a vibrant community.

Despite most members often learning instruments from scratch, our orchestra learns instruments of varying sizes, genres and choreography to bring engaging performances to our audiences. With each section having support from our experienced alumni members and instructors, NYPSO has improved by leaps and bounds over the years.
