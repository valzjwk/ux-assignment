Alumni

Where Your Journey Continues



Alumni Association (AA)

NYPSO has a strong Alumni Association made of over 30 active members that help out with training and behind-the-scenes action. From providing insightful feedback during weekly training to helping to network with other orchestras and organisers. With each alumni having a strong connection with NYP and NYPSO, they serve passionately and readily with the hope of helping current students grow their talent and spirit of loyalty towards the CCA and school.

NYPSO AA also perform at concerts and performances with other bands as Alumni Band, continuing on their passion despite their new and busy schedules. Apart from inspiring the current NYPSO members to strive to always do their best, they are also seen as role models, showcasing their love and devotion to music and the CCA even after graduation.

Events

NYP Career & Education Fair

Participating as a mentor during the fair, Alumnis can share their experience in the musical industry in Singapore and what steps aspiring students can take to reach their goal. Alumni can also share paths available for joining Alumni Band, coming to serve as NYPSO Alumni amidst the post-tertiary life.

Alumni Band Conference (ABC)

An annual concert held by various tertiary alumni bands with the passion to continue playing their instruments and performing even after graduation. ABC is held at different locations each year, with a different Alumni Band hosting every time.
